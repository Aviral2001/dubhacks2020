About DancerFlow

Our mission is to bring the world of dance right to you. Whether you are classically trained and want to brush up on your technique or a new dancer wanting to learn the next viral dance, DancerFlow was made for you. DancerFlow allows you to practice dance moves, routines, and trends right from your own home. With quick and accurate feedback, DancerFlow is your personal dance instructor.

To begin practicing your moves, pick from our selection of dance moves, routines, and viral dances or upload your own. Preview the move, then get to practicing! You can do the move in real time or upload a video of you practicing. Either way make sure you have a clear background and good lighting for best results. After seeing your moves, DancerFlow will give you feedback and track your progress. It’s that simple. Pick your move. Practice. Track your improvement. Now, get practicing! And remember to have fun!

-The DancerFlow Team
